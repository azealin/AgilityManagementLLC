About US

Mission Statement: Effective resources management to maximize profitability.

Our Business

A small business based in Phoenix AZ. Focusing in supporting construction industry in Project Management including control functions on capital projects. With over 20 years’ experience in the industry globally, the firm  offers thorough knowledge to identify the needs based on particular customer and recommend cost effective measures to ensure the project is executed as planned.

Changes occur during the project duration. By actively managing the contingency and adjust the project plan along the duration, the firm will recommend client actions to maximize the profitability on the project.

As of 9/10/09, our firm is now certified DBE (Disadvantaged Business Enterprise) in Project Management, under the Arizona Unified Certification Program (AZUCP). We are also certified as MBE (Minority-Owned Business Enterprise) and SBE (Small Business Enterprise) by the City of Phoenix.

 

Who We Are

The principal of the firm has 20+ years experience in heavy and utilities construction industry, including projects in:

  • Water distribution mains replacement in AZ and HI, from $0.2M to $2.5M 
  • Wastewater treatment plant upgrade in HI and NY, from $1M to $3B,
  • New Pump Station in NM,  $0.5M
  • Highway and wastewater collection system construction in Saudi Arabia, $5M to $20M

The principal served in various capacities as Project Manager, Estimator, Lead Cost Engineer, Civil Engineer, Surveyor

Licensed Professional Engineer in Civil Engineering in the State of Arizona

Member of AACE International, since 1990. Currently serve as the Director of Certification in the Phoenix Chapter.
